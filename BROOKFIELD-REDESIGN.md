## 📖 Brookfield Lessons Applied

### What We Learned from Brookfield:

1. **Bold, clear value propositions** - No jargon, immediate understanding
2. **Statistics build credibility** - Large numbers, prominent placement
3. **Themes tell a story** - Forward-looking, problem-solving focus
4. **Ecosystem differentiates** - Shows unique advantage of being part of network
5. **Audience segmentation** - Clear targeting of different stakeholder groups
6. **Premium language** - "Enduring value", "operational expertise", etc.
7. **Warm professionalism** - Not cold corporate, but sophisticated and approachable
8. **Visual polish** - High-quality images, clean layouts, generous spacing
